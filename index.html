01. The Problem

DeFi still asks users to act like pro traders.

Most people think in goals: safe yield, AI exposure, downside protection, or portfolio balance. Traditional DeFi forces them to translate those goals into manual swaps, LPs, routes, risk checks, and strategy decisions.

⚙️
PROBLEM_01

Execution overload

Users must understand routing, slippage, liquidity pools, impermanent loss, volatility, and yield risk before they can take action.

🧠
PROBLEM_02

Goals are not orders

A user says, “I want low-risk yield,” but most apps only accept exact trades or strategy choices. The intent layer is missing.

🔮
PROBLEM_03

Agents lack competition

Most bots are isolated black boxes. Intent-0n turns agents into a visible market where performance, fit, and risk can be compared.

02. The Solution

Write the intent. Let agents duel for the outcome.

Intent-0n is not a normal trading bot and not a normal yield farm. It is a coordination layer: users express goals, agents compete, and the best match becomes an execution preview.

Step 01🌙

Natural Intent

User describes the desired outcome in plain language.

Step 02⚔️

Agent Market

Specialized agents compete with different strategies.

Step 03🏆

Score Engine

The system scores fit by risk, goal, text signals, and agent metrics.

Step 04⚡

Jaine Route

The winner produces a simulated Jaine DEX execution path.

Step 05💾

0G Record

Future versions store intents, bids, outcomes, and reputation on 0G.

03. Intent Builder

The main feature: describe your intention.

Choices are useful, but natural language is the soul of Intent-0n. Tell the system what you actually want, then let the agents compete.

Intent text influences the mock scoring. Keywords like safe, protect, AI, alpha, copy, rebalance, or aggressive can push different agents higher.

04. Architecture

Designed for 0G + Jaine.

The prototype is UI-only today, but the future architecture is simple: 0G stores intent and agent data, agents produce competing solutions, and Jaine DEX acts as the execution and liquidity layer.

💾

0G data layer

Future storage for user intents, solver proposals, score history, execution records, and reputation proofs.

🤖

Agent network

Agents read intents and produce strategy proposals based on their skill modules, risk styles, and specialties.

Jaine DEX

Future execution and liquidity layer for swap routes, LP positions, and strategy settlement.

🏛️

Settlement future

Smart contracts can later handle staking, rewards, slashing, execution verification, and agent reputation.
